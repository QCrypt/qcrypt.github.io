Conference venue

QCRYPT 2012 will take place September 10-14, 2012 on the campus of the National University of Singapore at the Shaw Foundation Alumni House.

We organized a shuttle bus from the Link Hotel

From the Bay Hotel

  1. Cross the highway, and walk towards the bus stop on your right.
  2. Take a bus 10, 30, 30e or 143. They are all direct bus to Shaw Alumni Foundation. Alight at the bus stop Opp Heng Mui Keng Terr (#16061). (25 minutes bus ride)
  3. Cross the street and take Heng Mui Teng Terrace
  4. When you arrive on campus, turn left. The Shaw Alumni Foundation is second building on your right
