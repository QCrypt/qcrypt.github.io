Conference venue

QCrypt 2014 will take place at Telecom ParisTech:

Barrault site: 46, rue Barrault – 75013 Paris

  • Subway: Take the line 6 and stop at Corvisart station
  • RER : Take the RER B until Denfert-Rochereau station. Connection in Denfert Rochereau and take the subway line 6
  • Bus : line 62 (Vergniaud), 21 (Daviel) or 67 (Bobillot)
  • Vélib’ : stations 13022 (27 & 36, rue de la Butte aux Cailles), 13048 (20, rue Wurtz) or 13024 (81, rue Bobillot)
