Conference Venue

Posted on August 22, 2014February 5, 2018 by admin

QCrypt 2018 will take place at Shanghai International Conference Center, Shanghai, China:

 

Located in the central Shanghai, the hall has a very good access from the Lujiazui metro stations.

It holds maximum 700 people in the fully equipped main Hall, and adjoining are small to middle sized meeting rooms that are open to participants.

From there, you can reach to the Oriental Pearl TV Tower in 10 minutes on foot.

 

Address:

No. 2727 Binjiang Avenue, Shanghai, China

 

Access:

By Shanghai Metro

5 minutes walk from Lujiazui station of Line 2
