Call for Proposals to Host QCrypt 2020 – Deadline 1 November 2018

The QCrypt steering committee invites proposals to host QCrypt 2020.

According to the current rotation of continents, proposals from Europe are preferred.

This will be handled by a 2-step process.

  1. If you are interested, please send a letter of intent to
    <qcrypt-steer@googlegroups.com> by Nov. 1, 2018, with the following information:
    * Name and affiliation of main organizer
    * Possibilities of conference dates (preferably in late August 2020)
    * Location and size of intended conference venue (depending on the location, the conference venue should have a capacity of at least 300-400)
  2. The steering committee will ask the top contenders to work out a full proposal.

Thank you!
