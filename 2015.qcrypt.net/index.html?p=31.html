    Hotels around the venue

    There are hotel accommodations around the venue. Some of them are listed below for your information.
    We advise that you book the hotel at your earliest opportunity to secure accommodation, as accommodations in Tokyo tend to get quite scarce recently. Please click the numbers on the map or the hotel names and access respective web pages for further information.

    If you wish to check or reserve hotels other than the above, there are some websites that you might find useful.

    1. https://www.jnto.go.jp/ (Japan National Tourism Organization)
    2. http://www.japanican.com/en/hotel/ (JTB Global Marketing & Travel Inc)
