    College Accommodation

    It is also pleasant to stay in one of the nearby Colleges of the University of Cambridge.
    Please note that Colleges offer Bed & Breakfast style accommodation (ie without the facilities of the big hotels) but provide an opportunity to stay in one of the Cambridge Colleges at a below hotel rate.

    There has been no block booking of College rooms, so should you wish to book a room, you are advised to do so early, as these are likely to sell out well before the conference.
